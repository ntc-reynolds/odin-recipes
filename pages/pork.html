Pork Tenderloin

Description:

A deliciously moist and flavorful pork tenderloin recipe, perfect for a special occasion or a weeknight dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 1 (1-1.5 pound) pork tenderloin
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup white wine (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400째F (200째C).
  2. Rinse the pork tenderloin and pat dry with paper towels.
  3. Rub the tenderloin with olive oil, honey, Dijon mustard, thyme, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. Heat a large oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Sear the tenderloin for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until browned.
  5. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and roast for 15-20 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 145째F (63째C).
  6. While the pork is roasting, prepare the glaze by mixing chicken broth and white wine (if using). Brush the glaze over the pork during the last 5 minutes of roasting.
  7. Remove the pork from the oven and let it rest for 5-10 minutes before slicing.
  8. Slice the pork tenderloin and serve with your favorite sides, such as roasted vegetables or mashed potatoes.

Tips:

  • Use a meat thermometer to ensure the pork reaches a safe internal temperature.
  • Let the pork rest for at least 5 minutes before slicing to retain juiciness.
  • Consider using a cast-iron skillet for even heat distribution and a crispy crust.
